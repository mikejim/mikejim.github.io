Research profile

Miguel Jimeno is an Associate Professor of the Systems Engineering Department at Universidad del Norte, in Barranquilla, Colombia. has worked for Universidad del Norte since 2010 after graduating from the Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Computer Science Department at University of South Florida. .
